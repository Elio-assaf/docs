## What is Optiverse?

Optiverse is a comprehensive meeting assistant platform that uses advanced AI to transform how your team conducts, documents, and follows up on meetings. From real-time transcription to intelligent summaries and automated action items, Optiverse ensures no important detail is ever lost.
