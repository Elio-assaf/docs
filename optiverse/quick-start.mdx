Get your Optiverse meeting assistant up and running in just 5 minutes! This guide will walk you through the essential steps to start transforming your meetings.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have:
- A valid Optiverse account
- Access to your preferred meeting platform (Google Meet, Zoom, etc.)
- Administrative access to integrate with your tools (optional)

## Troubleshooting Quick Start Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can't access my dashboard">
    - Check your internet connection
    - Clear browser cache and cookies
    - Try incognito/private browsing mode
    - [Contact support](/optiverse/support/contact-support) if issues persist
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="AI assistant not joining meetings">
    - Verify meeting platform integration is enabled
    - Check that you've granted necessary permissions
    - Ensure meeting is properly scheduled in Optiverse
    - Review our [common issues guide](/optiverse/support/common-issues)
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Integrations not working">
    - Confirm you have admin access to the external tool
    - Check that OAuth permissions were granted
    - Verify your account has the necessary subscription level
    - Try disconnecting and reconnecting the integration
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
