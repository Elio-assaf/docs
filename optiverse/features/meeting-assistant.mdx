Optiverse's AI Meeting Assistant is your intelligent companion that transforms how you experience meetings. It actively participates in your discussions, capturing insights, managing tasks, and providing real-time assistance to make every meeting more productive.

## What is the AI Meeting Assistant?

The AI Meeting Assistant is an advanced AI system that:
- **Listens actively** to your meeting conversations
- **Understands context** and meeting dynamics
- **Extracts key insights** automatically
- **Manages tasks** and follow-ups
- **Provides real-time assistance** during meetings

### Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Poor Transcription Quality">
    **Common Causes:**
    - Background noise interference
    - Multiple people speaking simultaneously
    - Poor microphone quality
    - Accented or fast speech
    
    **Solutions:**
    - Use noise-canceling microphones
    - Implement speaking protocols
    - Adjust AI sensitivity settings
    - Provide speaker training
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Missed Action Items">
    **Common Causes:**
    - Unclear task assignments
    - Implicit rather than explicit commitments
    - Complex or multi-part tasks
    - Informal language patterns
    
    **Solutions:**
    - Use explicit task language
    - Confirm assignments verbally
    - Break down complex tasks
    - Review and edit AI suggestions
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
