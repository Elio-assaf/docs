### Best Practices

<Warning>
**Privacy and Ethics**: Always respect participant privacy and use analytics to improve team performance, not to surveil or penalize individuals.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Focus on Trends, Not Events">
    **Pattern Recognition**
    - Look for consistent patterns over time
    - Avoid overreacting to single meeting data
    - Consider external factors affecting performance
    - Use data to guide, not dictate decisions
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Combine Quantitative and Qualitative">
    **Holistic Analysis**
    - Supplement metrics with team feedback
    - Consider context behind the numbers
    - Include participant input in analysis
    - Balance efficiency with team satisfaction
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Act on Insights">
    **Implementation Focus**
    - Turn insights into actionable changes
    - Test improvements systematically
    - Measure impact of changes
    - Iterate based on results
  </Step>
</Steps>
