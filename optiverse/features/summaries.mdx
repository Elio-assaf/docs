## Summary Formats

### Standard Formats

<Accordion>
  <AccordionItem title="Meeting Overview">
    **Structured Summary**
    
    **Format Structure:**
    ```
    Meeting: [Title]
    Date: [Date and Time]
    Participants: [Attendee List]
    Duration: [Meeting Length]
    
    Key Outcomes:
    • Decision 1
    • Decision 2
    • Decision 3
    
    Action Items:
    • Task 1 - Assigned to [Name] - Due [Date]
    • Task 2 - Assigned to [Name] - Due [Date]
    
    Next Steps:
    • Follow-up meeting scheduled
    • Key milestones identified
    ```
  </AccordionItem>
  
  <AccordionItem title="Executive Brief">
    **Leadership Focus**
    
    **Executive Format:**
    ```
    Executive Summary: [Meeting Title]
    
    Strategic Decisions:
    • [Major decision with business impact]
    • [Resource allocation decisions]
    
    Key Risks & Opportunities:
    • [Risk identification and mitigation]
    • [Opportunity assessment]
    
    Resource Requirements:
    • [Budget implications]
    • [Personnel needs]
    
    Timeline & Milestones:
    • [Critical deadlines]
    • [Key deliverables]
    ```
  </AccordionItem>
