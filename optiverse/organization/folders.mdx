## Best Practices

### Folder Naming Conventions

<Accordion>
  <AccordionItem title="Consistent Naming">
    **Standardized Approach**
    
    **Naming Patterns:**
    ```
    Format: [Type] - [Subject] - [Date/Period]
    
    Examples:
    - "Project - Mobile App - Q4 2024"
    - "Client - Acme Corp - 2024"
    - "Team - Engineering - Weekly"
    - "Event - Product Launch - Dec 2024"
    ```
    
    **Best Practices:**
    - Use clear, descriptive names
    - Include relevant dates
    - Maintain consistent formatting
    - Avoid special characters
  </AccordionItem>
  
  <AccordionItem title="Hierarchical Structure">
    **Logical Organization**
    
    **Recommended Hierarchy:**
    ```
    Company
    ├── Departments
    │   ├── Engineering
    │   │   ├── Team Meetings
    │   │   ├── Project Reviews
    │   │   └── One-on-Ones
    │   └── Sales
    │       ├── Team Meetings
    │       ├── Client Meetings
    │       └── Pipeline Reviews
    ├── Projects
    │   ├── Active Projects
    │   ├── Completed Projects
    │   └── On Hold
    └── Clients
        ├── Enterprise Clients
        ├── SMB Clients
        └── Prospects
    ```
  </AccordionItem>
</Accordion>
