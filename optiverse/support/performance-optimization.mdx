<Steps>
  <Step title="Slow Meeting Join">

## AI Processing Optimization

### Transcription Performance

<Warning>
**Audio Quality Impact**: Poor audio quality significantly affects transcription speed and accuracy. Ensure optimal microphone setup for best performance.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Audio Setup">
    **Optimal Configuration**
    - Use high-quality microphone
    - Minimize background noise
    - Speak clearly and at normal pace
    - Test audio levels before meetings
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Processing Settings">
    **AI Configuration**
    - Adjust accuracy vs. speed settings
    - Configure language preferences
    - Optimize for meeting type
    - Enable hardware acceleration
  </Step>
</Steps>
